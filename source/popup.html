With this chrome extension you can automatically join giveaways on Gameminer.

  1. Go to gameminer.net and log in with your Steam account.
  2. Click on "Bot enabled" in this settings window and confirm with "Save".
  3. Refresh the website and the bot will start running.

Note: You need to keep the website open in order to keep the bot running.

This might be changed in the future.
